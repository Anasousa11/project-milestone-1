About Us

Welcome to Pebble Path Montessori, a small, nurturing nursery in the heart of Billericay, Essex, where children aged 6 months to 4 years are guided with care, respect, and curiosity.

Founded in 2019 by Ana Sousa, an experienced Montessori educator and passionate advocate for child-led learning, our nursery was born from a desire to create a truly child-focused environment where the unique rhythm of every child is honoured. Over the past five years, we've built a warm and inclusive community where families feel supported and children thrive.

At Pebble Path, we follow the time-tested principles of Dr. Maria Montessori, believing that children flourish when given the freedom to explore within clear and thoughtful boundaries. Our approach nurtures independence, confidence, and a lifelong love of learning—starting from the earliest days of infancy.
